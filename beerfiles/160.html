Poured a very light hay color. White head was only slight, and dissipated very rapidly. Plenty of large, slow moving carbonation bubbles. Light lacing.

The aroma was quite fruity, and had an overwhelming note of banana. Also had a slight spice aroma similar to allspice. No overly powerful aroma of malt or hops.

Very weak flavor reminded me of drinking a very lighlty flavored glass of water. Aftertaste is reminiscient of drinking a macrobrew. A second mouthful does have a slightly more malted taste then the first.

Very watery feel in my mouth. I appreciated the carbonation, but didn't make up for the overall lack of body.

I can't recommend this beer to anyone. While it looks and smells quite pleasent, the taste leaves much to be desired. A definite miss from a brewery I generally like.

I picked up a single of this beer to try. I am so glad that I only bought a single...

I had this out of a 12 oz brown bottle and served in an imperial pint glass. Bottling # 4 199.

Pours a straw yellow color with a bone white head that vanishes way too fast. No traces of lacings at all. Some bubbly carbonation.

Smells grainy and grassy with a faint whiff of hops in the background.

This beer tastes more like an attempt at a Pilsner than an American Blonde Ale. Grainy and sweet taste with a very dry finish. Feels clean and crisp on the palate. Sweet malts with a dry snap at the end. Overall, it's a watered and dumbed down beer.

An okay beer, but not worth recommending or seeking out.

12 oz brown bottle.

1/2" frothy white head that leads to some thin lacing that doesn't hold well. Pale yellow straw in color; looks very thin and watery.

Light malt sweetness. Faint pear and apple aromas. Very subtle aromatics.

An initial sweetness that gives way to a wheat like flavor. No fruit-like qualities at all. Virtually no bitterness which is surprising given the initial sweet and grain flavors. Nothing to write home about in terms of taste.

Very well rounded on the palate. Tastes thicker than it appears. Medium bodied.

Very clean, light, and low in terms of abv%. Easily consumable but not one I would want to drink as a session beer.

S - Perfect balance of vanilla and earthy with graininess and some chocolate.

Picked up a 32 ounce growler at tasting room in Tampa. Drank the next day. Not a great looking beer in the sense that it had some foamy head but it dissipated quickly and did not leave lacing around the glass. The smell is outstanding, you get the nut, chocolate, and even caramel with the strong vanilla, smells delicious. The taste follows the smell very much. Plenty of sweet malty nut flavor with the vanilla, along with a touch of caramel and chocolate. The feel is maybe a little thin, but very clean. Overall an extremely tasty beer with loads of flavor that is clean, smooth, and balanced. Kudos Cigar City.

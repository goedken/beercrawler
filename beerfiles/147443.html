12 oz. Can

Pours a very nice clean/clear pale yellow color, very nice carbonation, with a very nice thick/creamy/foamy two-finger plus white head, which leaves some pretty nice sticky lacing behind. The nose is hoppy, malty, slight yeasty, with some citrus, licoricey. The taste is nice, hoppy, malty, yeasty, citrusy, kinda licoricey. Medium body. Overall, quite a different kind of IPA to me, but still tasty.

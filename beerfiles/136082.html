BillRoth, Jan 01, 2016
4.09/5  rDev -7.3%
look: 4.5 | smell: 4.25 | taste: 4 | feel: 4 | overall: 4

From 22 oz bomber to Teku glass

A: Clear, clean, light golden-tan appearance. A small, wispy quickly dissipates leaving no lacing

S: Juicy bouquet as common with the Showers series. Lots of zesty citrus as well, but Galaxy yields a lot of tropical notes (reminds me of Nelson hops a bit). Dank skunkiness as well, all propelled by ample alcohol volatiles.

T/M: The tropical notes carry onto the palate, but this is more bitter and generically 'hoppy' than expected. Juicy as well, even nectar-y as the brew has a chewy complexion for the style. Pithy citrus, passion fruit and more subtly pineapple combine with a bold malt-backbone, leaving a hint of bitter residuals between sips. Warming booze kicks in toward the finish, which carries a relatively long close.

Not as tasty as Citrus or Mosaic showers, but those set a really high bar.

22 ounce Bomber into a Pint Glass..yeah so what!?

Reviewing another brew today, I thought I reviewed this before...guess I haven't. Well let's get it going then. For the start this brew pours Fantastic! Very dense deep Blonde body going on Light Amber in the sunlight. Looks like a Sunset. Crowned with a soapy white head that stood around for a while. This looks like what a DIPA should Look Like!

This Aroma is Dank! Dank as in Dense Fruity and Earth! The first wave of the aroma I pick up Ripe Sweet Peaches and Mango. Like opening a can of Delmonte Peaches and Mangos in heavy syrup. The second wave brings that Marijuana earthiness like a bag of Peach Kush...or something on that lines. Mild Citrus in the background. This brew smells Amazeballs!

The flavor is just as Banging. This brew brings it's Peachy ass over your place and makes itself comfy on your Palate. Then subtle Citric Orange Meat greets you with his Mango girlfriend....it's time for a Foursome in your mouth baby! Sweet Pale Malts puts the hops into check only so subtle that keeps it a beer and not hop water. This finishes dry with a dense bitterness that grabs the sides of the palate cutting the malt sweetness with ease. Lingering Oranges, Mango and Quality Weed in the aftertaste. The feel of the brew is Medium-Light on the palate with crisp Carbonation.

Overall: This Hop Variety is For me! The malt bill Sweetens this hop enough to let you know what it's about. A damn fine job it does! The Galaxy Hop is a Beast all its own...yet Galaxy and Citra would make an amazing DIPA. I don't know why this brew is rated as low as it is. I find no Faults with it. This and Fiddlehead's Mastermind made me a true believer in the Galaxy hop. This brew is simply amazing. This brew is Recommended!

Look: Black as night. Pours with a foamy head that fades within seconds leaving a khaki colored outer ring.

Smell: Bourbon and coffee hits right away. It's a sweeter nose than expected.

Taste: I was surprised here. The first things that hit me was a bust of vanilla and oak. Coffee is subdued a bit from the time in the bottle but it was still there. Bourbon is also fading but again, still present. Flavors come in waves.

Feel: Thick, creamy, stick, oily...however you want to describe it, its amazing. Probably the best part of this beer is the feel and that's impressive considering it tastes and smells amazing.

Overall: Easily the best BA Speedway for me and I've had a ton. Recently had a '13 BASS and this crushes it. I'm excited to get my hands on a '16 or two and let one sit for a bit like this one. This was one of the best beers I've ever had.

eifster82, Sep 04, 2016
4.68/5  rDev +0.2%
look: 4.75 | smell: 4.75 | taste: 4.5 | feel: 5 | overall: 4.75

I know this is the only version of Speedway that got the perfect 100, but I was (very sadly) a bit underwhelmed. It was still phenomenal, and I'm really glad I tried it, but I would say the regular, or BBA regular was still my preference.

Appearance: lighter in color, a tad more translucent than regular SS. A bit lighter brown, shinier, looked a little thinner (but definitely didn't taste any thinner!)

Smell: a lot of bourbon, really hid the Vietnamese coffee more than I would've liked. Bourbon really overtakes the nose, although over a firm foundation of aromatic coffee/stouty/thick Speedway.

Taste: Here's where I really wanted a little more - it really tasted like just BBA speedway, the vietnamese coffee wasn't really present. If anything, I could taste a more bitter and stronger aftertaste, which would've been great if it has been accompanied by strong vientamese coffee notes, but I think it just got lost in the sauce in this one.

Mouthfeel: still spectacular. thick, creamy, bold, heavy, full-bodied...Speedway really captures my ideal mouthfeel for a big ol' stout. just perfect.

Overall: glad I got to knock off another 100 Beer Advocate beer off my list, but I would hesitate to say it's the best version. Still, buy it whenever you can, it's worth it.
